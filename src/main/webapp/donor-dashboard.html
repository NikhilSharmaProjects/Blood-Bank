Donation Guidelines

  • Age should be between 18-65 years
  • Weight should be at least 50 kg
  • Hemoglobin level should be 12.5 g/dL or above
  • Wait at least 3 months between donations
  • Be in good health and well-rested
  • Avoid alcohol 24 hours before donation
  • Eat a healthy meal before donating
